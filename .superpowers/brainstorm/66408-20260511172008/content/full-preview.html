§1. Introduction

The dominant sequence transduction models are based on complex recurrent or convolutional neural networks that include an encoder and a decoder. The best performing models also connect the encoder and decoder through an attention mechanism.

Attention(Q, K, V) = softmax(QKT / √dk) V

In this work we propose the Transformer, a model architecture eschewing recurrence and instead relying entirely on an attention mechanism to draw global dependencies between input and output. The Transformer allows for significantly more parallelization and can achieve state-of-the-art translation quality after being trained for as little as twelve hours on eight P100 GPUs.

"Attention 是一种让模型关注输入重要部分的机制，就像在嘈杂的房间里把注意力集中到一个人的声音上。"
